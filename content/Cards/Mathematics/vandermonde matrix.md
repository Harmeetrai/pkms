---

$$
{\displaystyle V=V(x_{0},x_{1},\cdots ,x_{m})={\begin{bmatrix}1&x_{0}&x_{0}^{2}&\dots &x_{0}^{n}\\1&x_{1}&x_{1}^{2}&\dots &x_{1}^{n}\\1&x_{2}&x_{2}^{2}&\dots &x_{2}^{n}\\\vdots &\vdots &\vdots &\ddots &\vdots \\1&x_{m}&x_{m}^{2}&\dots &x_{m}^{n}\end{bmatrix}}}
